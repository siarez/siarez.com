What you just experienced is a demo a project where I trained a model to detect nods. The model was created and trained in Pytorch and then ported to Deeplearn.js to run in the browser.

The green bar is the probability of a nod as inferred by the model. When the bar extends past the black notch, we can be fairly certain that we have had a nod.

There is a slight (~1.5s) delay between a nod and its detection.
p.s. The demo usually runs smoother in Chrome.
